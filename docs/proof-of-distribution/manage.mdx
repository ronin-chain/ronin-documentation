---
title: Manage your builder profile
description: Add contracts after activation, change admin or treasury wallets, and claim rewards.
---

After activation, the dashboard switches to a management view for
adding contracts, changing wallets, and claiming rewards.

## Add contracts after activation

Same flow as during registration (`Add Contract` → paste → verify via
any method; see [Verify contract ownership](/proof-of-distribution/verify)). Once verified,
the contract appears in `Ready to register`.

## Push deployer-verified contracts on-chain

If the new contract is deployer-verified, click `Add to registry` on
its card in the `Ready to register` tab and sign the transaction in
your wallet. The contract moves to `Registered` when the transaction
confirms.

## Contracts verified by other methods

Contracts verified by owner, access-control, Safe, or Sky Mavis manual
review are added to your tracking list by Sky Mavis. The card reads
`Awaiting registration` until that happens.

## Change your admin wallet

In the Project panel, open the three-dots menu and click
`Transfer admin…`. Paste the new admin wallet address and sign in your
current admin wallet.

:::warning Transferring admin is one-way and permanent

Once the transaction confirms, the **previous admin wallet is
permanently retired** for this builder profile **and can never be a
builder admin under PoD again** (not for this profile, not for a new
one). The new wallet immediately holds the admin role. If you set the
new admin to an address you don't actually control, you've effectively
burned your profile. Double-check the address before signing.

:::

## Change your treasury wallet

In the Project panel, open the three-dots menu and click
`Change treasury…`. Paste the new address and sign. Treasury changes
are far less consequential than admin changes; you can change treasury
again later if needed, and rewards already paid out are unaffected.

## Edit project info

Project name, description, logo, website, and X URL live off-chain.
Click `Edit Project` to open the form, change what you need, and
click `Save`. No transaction is required; the changes are immediate.

## Claim rewards

Accumulated rewards appear in the Builder Status panel. The panel shows
your claimable balance, a `Claim` button, and a history of past claims
with explorer links so you can reconcile against on-chain accounting.
Click `Claim` to submit the claim transaction. Rewards are paid in RON
to your treasury wallet, not the admin wallet.
